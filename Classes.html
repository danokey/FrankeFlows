Private & Small Group Classes at Sanctuary

Hannah also hosts private yoga sessions and small group classes at Sanctuary Studios in Seattle. Sanctuary is a specialized and customizable studio in Lower Queen Anne, Seattle. Customization includes heat, scent, and class. This is great for the newbie yogi wanting a hands-on-teaching experience, an advanced practitioner wanting to learn specific skills, or a group of friends/family who want a special experience together. If you would like to learn more, book a free 15 minute consultation with Hannah to learn more. Prices vary based on number of students.
