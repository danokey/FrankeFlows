FrankeFlows Virtual Studio

FrankeFlows Virtual Yoga Studio is a Seattle-based, owned and operated female venture. Born from the closures of yoga studios nationwide due to COVID-19, the Virtual Studio aims to build connection and and community from wherever you are through communal breath and movement. Our studio currently hosts eight (8) classes a week that range from Vinyasa Yoga, Yin Yoga, and the upbeat Yoga Sculpt. We hope to move and groove with you soon on your virtual mat!
