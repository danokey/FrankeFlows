Daily Schedule

Expect to begin each day with an invigorating Vinyasa flow filled with breath, movement, and joy. Then in the evenings embark on a journey inward for a Yin/Restorative practice to help release tension and process insights of your day.

In between yoga practices find adventure or rest. There will be plenty of free time to visit the oceanfront spa, visit the pool, go snorkeling, kayak, hang at the bar, hike, and more!

Cuisine

Each day, three nourishing meals will be provided and are included in your investment. These meals are Mexican inspired, sourced locally, and served buffet style. All dietary restrictions can be accommodated (vegetarian, vegan, gluten free, etc.)
