Franke Flows On-Demand Library

The FrankeFlows Virtual Studio was born from the closures of yoga studios nationwide due to COVID-19. Classes were taught on zoom and recorded by Hannah and her amazing team of talented Seattle based yoga instructors. After over a year of providing live virtual yoga classes the team has compiled many of our favorite classes in the below on-demand library. Feel free to continue to flow with us online in perpetuity!

Options include buying a specific class or signing up for an all access membership for unlimited classes for only $15 a month. New classes will continue to be added.
